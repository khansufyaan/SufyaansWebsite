Our Mission

100% Halal or Nothing at All

Mirchi Cafe believes in bringing quality craft and ingredients to the halal food scene. We are a family run business who sees our customers and employees as an extension of our family. We strive to serve the community through charity and outreach events. Our focus is to create an experience centered around building bridges between comfort and understanding of the Pakistani culture through flavors, art and music.
